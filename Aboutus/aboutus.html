Foodstart was founded by a group of multimedia Arts students from De La Salle-College of Saint Benilde. Their reason behind Foodstart is to help small food business with advertising their products. Since the pandemic, with many constraints, social media platforms and websites are the ways to promote the products of the food businesses. And based on their research and study, good product visual designs affect consumer decisions. Having appealing images for clients is beneficial to the business's sale, especially if the visuals are well-designed and crafted to stand out among competitors and that is where Foodstart comes in to give you a helping hand.
